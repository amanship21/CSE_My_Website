Your Learning Path

A clear progression from total beginner to production-ready sysadmin.

01

Get Comfortable in the Shell

Log in, look around, run commands. Understand what a shell is and why it's powerful.

bashterminalnavigation~1 week
02

Master File Operations

Files are everything in Linux. Learn to create, find, read, move, and delete them at speed.

lscpfindgrep~1 week
03

Understand Permissions

chmod, chown, and the user/group model. Know exactly why things fail and how to fix them.

chmodchownsudo~5 days
04

Text Fu: grep, awk & sed

The ability to transform text data on the command line is a superpower. Build it here.

grepawksedpipe~2 weeks
05

Automate with Bash Scripts

Write your first real scripts. Variables, loops, conditionals, cron jobs, and automation.

bash scriptingcronfunctions~2 weeks
06

Networking & SSH

Connect to remote servers, transfer files securely, diagnose network issues, and harden SSH.

sshscpnetstatiptables~2 weeks
